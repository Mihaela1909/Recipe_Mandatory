The minds behind the recipes

MEET THE HEAD CHEF

Arthur Raff

Arthur Raff is a culinary artist whose journey began with a simple yet profound love for food, transforming it into a passion for innovation in the professional kitchen. With a style that marries classic techniques with bold, global flavors, Chef Raff has quickly established a reputation for creating dishes that are both comforting and unexpected. His dedication to using fresh, high-quality ingredients shines through in every plate, embodying his philosophy that the best food tells a story. Driven by creativity and a relentless pursuit of excellence, Chef Raff stands ready to invite you to experience the mind and heart behind his unforgettable recipes.

MEET THE SOUS CHEF

Victoria Jenkins

Victoria Jenkins is the indispensable force and culinary backbone of the kitchen, serving as the trusted Sous Chef. Her journey in the culinary world is defined by precision, dedication, and an unshakeable calm under pressure. Trained in rigorous European techniques, Victoria possesses an encyclopedic knowledge of execution, ensuring every dish leaving the pass meets the highest standards of perfection. Known for her expert leadership and ability to seamlessly manage the rhythm of a fast-paced environment, she is not only a master technician but also a mentor to the junior staff. Victoria Jenkins embodies the spirit of professionalism, making her the essential partner in bringing the kitchen's creative visions to life.
